🎯 Keyword Tiers — How Keywords Are Targeted

Every keyword in your queue is classified by how close it is to ranking. The tier tells you the expected effort and timeline.

⚡
Quick Win Position 11–20
You already rank. One strong content update pushes you to page 1. Fastest ROI — expect results in days to weeks.
📈
Short Term Position 21–35
You're ranking weakly. A focused new post or page rewrite builds relevance. Expect results in 4–8 weeks.
🎯
Long Term Position 36–100
Barely visible. New content from scratch builds authority over time. Expect results in 2–4 months.
🚨
Priority Gap Seed list — not in GSC
Competitors rank here, you don't appear at all. Manually curated keywords you want to own. Highest strategic value.

📋 Approval Types — What Each Item Means

META UPDATE Rewrites the SEO title and meta description for an existing page. Low risk, high CTR impact. Always approve these.
PAGE UPDATE Rewrites the full content of an existing page to push a ranking keyword into the top 10. Review content before approving.
BLOG DRAFT A brand new blog post targeting a content gap keyword. Check the voice score badge — aim for 7+ before publishing.
NEW PAGE A new landing page for a location or service keyword. Requires your developer to set up the WordPress page parent correctly.
ON-PAGE EDIT A specific structural improvement to an existing page — adding a comparison table, fixing a heading, adding internal links.
REVIEW REPLY A suggested response to a Google review. Drafted in brand voice. Always personalise before publishing.

✦ Brand Voice Scoring

Every blog draft and page update is scored 1–10 for brand voice accuracy before it reaches your queue. A second Claude pass checks for generic phrases, banned words, AI tells like em dashes, and whether the content could only have been written for Pickl or Bonbird.

9–10 On Brand Sharp, specific, unmistakably the brand. Approve with confidence.
7–8 On Brand Mostly strong. Minor generic moments. Fine to approve.
5–6 Review Voice Some brand voice but generic in places. Use Rewrite with AI or edit manually.
1–4 Off Brand Does not sound like the brand. Rewrite before publishing.
< 5 Auto-rejected Content scored below 5 is never queued. You'll see an error in the scheduler logs.
